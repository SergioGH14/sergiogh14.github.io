Hello world, I'm Sergio Gómez
Born and raised in Vila-real, I pursued my studies at the Polytechnic University of Valencia (UPV). After spending six enriching years in Valencia, I moved to Barcelona with my partner to pursue our professional growth together. My passion extends beyond backend programming to encompass history and science. In my leisure time, I often find myself programming or indulging in educational videos related to these subjects. Recently, I have developed an interest in specialty coffee and custom keyboards, though a good beer and patatas bravas always have a special place in my heart. I am more of a dog person and have a lovely dog of my own, though I hold a deep affection for all animals. I believe in speaking up when I have something valuable to contribute, and I thrive in collaborative team environments.
